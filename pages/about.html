Bache & Co.

Built for collectors who want clean terms and straightforward buying.

Bache & Co. focuses on raw Brilliant Uncirculated (BU) Morgan and Peace silver dollars. v0 is intentionally simple: limited SKUs, transparent pricing, and an ordering flow designed to reduce disputes.

What we sell (v0)

  • Raw BU Morgan & Peace dollars — Our Choice (dates/mints vary)
  • Clear formula pricing tied to spot silver + tiered premium
  • Optional quotes for specific dates/mints (as available)

What we don’t do (v0)

  • No “MS63 guaranteed” type promises (raw BU is not a slabbed grade)
  • No confusing checkout gimmicks
  • No surprise terms

Why reservation-style ordering

Silver moves. Fraud happens. A reservation-style order lets us confirm pricing, verify details, and keep operations clean — especially for larger quantities.
